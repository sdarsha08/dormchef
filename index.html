Pick Your Meal

Mac &
Cheese
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup elbow macaroni - 1/2 cup water - 1/4 cup milk - 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese - Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the macaroni and water. 2. Microwave on high for 2 minutes, stir, and then microwave in 1-minute intervals, stirring each time, until the pasta is cooked (about 5-6 minutes total). Add more water if needed. 3. Stir in the milk and cheese, then microwave for an additional 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and creamy. 4. Season with salt and pepper and enjoy.
Vege
Stir Fry
Ingredients:
- 1 1 cup mixed frozen vegetables - 1 cup cooked rice (microwavable rice packs work great) - 1 tablespoon soy sauce - 1 teaspoon sesame oil or olive oil - Salt and pepper to taste - Optional: cooked chicken, tofu, or scrambled egg for added protein
Instructions:
1. Place the frozen vegetables in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave for 2-3 minutes until heated through. 2. Add the cooked rice to the bowl and mix with the vegetables. 3. Stir in the soy sauce and sesame oil, then microwave for another 1-2 minutes until everything is heated through. 4. Season with salt and pepper and add any optional protein if desired.
Tortilla
Pizza
Ingredients:
- 1 whole wheat tortilla - 2 tablespoons marinara sauce or pizza sauce - 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese - Toppings of choice (pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, mushrooms, etc.)
Instructions:
1. Place the tortilla on a microwave-safe plate. 2. Spread the marinara sauce evenly over the tortilla. 3. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the sauce and add your favorite toppings. 4. Microwave on high for 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. 5. Cut into slices and enjoy.
